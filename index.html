DR. APJ ABDUL KALAM

APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, on October 15, 1931. He studied physics and aerospace engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, and the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO). He was a pioneer in the development of India's nuclear and missile programs. He was also a recipient of the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.
